Who is Homer Simpsons?

He is part of the most loveable family in America or the possibly the world.

He is the patriarch of a blue collar family in Springfield, America. Together with his family they have made television history, earning multiple emmy awards. Eating is his passion. He loves to eat donuts. His best skill is finding ways to do as little as possible without getting caught. He once bowl a perfect score of 300.

His family members include:

  • Wife: Marge
  • Son: Bart
  • Daughter: Lisa
  • Youngest Daughter: Maggie
